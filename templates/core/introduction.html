Creation of retrospection

Creating retrospective has four parts.

The first part is "What did I learn?". You will be asked about what you learned. There will be form for each entry. You can submit as many entries, as you want.

The second part is about failures. Failures are painful, but very valuable if you consider retrospection.

The third part is pretty sweet, you fill a form about your successes. As before, you can submit as many entries that you want.

Last part is a bit general. You write general retrospection, there are two sections: summary and directions.

After it you would be able to review your retrospection and submit it or not. It's not possible yet, to change entries you submitted before.

Projects

It's important to create projects before you create retrospection, because successes and failures are directly bound to projects. And without projects, you wouldn't be able to create any of those entries.
