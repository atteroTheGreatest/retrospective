Creation of retrospection

Creating a retrospective consists of four parts.

The first is "What did I learn?". You will be asked about what you learned. There will be a form for each entry. You can submit as many entries as you want.

The second part is about failures. Failures are painful, but very valuable when considering a retrospective.

The third part is pretty sweet, you fill a form about your successes. As before, you can submit as many entries as you need.

Last part is a bit more general. You write a general retrospective, which is made of two sections: summary and directions.

After it you would be able to review your retrospective and submit it or not. It's not possible yet, to change entries you submitted before.

Projects

It's important to create projects before you create a retrospective, because successes and failures are directly bound to projects. And without projects, you wouldn't be able to create any of those entries.
